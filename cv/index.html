I started in my rural hometown studying certifications, learning by breaking and fixing systems, then I moved into real TelCo datacenters in Turin and Milan. Those years shaped both my technical and personal growth: complex projects, talented people, deep troubleshooting, and the occasional 3 a.m. rollback.

I followed my flow and joined a top hardware vendor as a System Engineer, designing and presenting server architectures at scale within a high-performance, strongly aligned and caring team. After deciding to leave Milan, I moved to a leading Managed Service Provider, working on a Tier IV datacenter and designing multi-vendor infrastructures across compute, storage, virtualization, and data protection, managing the full presales lifecycle end-to-end.

Today I'm transforming my homelab into an AI inference engineering lab running on a PowerEdge R730 and a dedicated RTX 4070 Super GPU node. I design, benchmark, and optimize LLM serving workloads daily using vLLM, LMCache, Docker, and a full Prometheus/Grafana/DCGM observability stack.

Curiosity and human connections guide both my work and my personal journey.
