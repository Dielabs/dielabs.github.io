// about

Dielabs is a personal inference engineering lab focused on the operational and infrastructural side of AI systems. No training pipelines, no data science — just runtimes, memory, throughput and observability.

Built on 10+ years of enterprise infrastructure background (VMware, Nutanix, NetApp), reoriented around GPU-accelerated inference stacks and the engineering discipline required to run them well.
